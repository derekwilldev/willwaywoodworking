Welcome to Will Way Woodworking!

I am Tyler Will, and I can be found in Columbus, Ohio. My art, and my passion, is wood crafting, and this website showcases my various experience and projects in Fine Woodworking.

My goal is to always produce exceptionally handcrafted woodworking creations while continually learning new techniques and gaining as much experience as possible. Using the experience and education I have gained through school, various projects, and hard work in the industry, I deliver high quality creations and I am grateful to have a career doing what I love most.

The "cut of my grain"

Creating household or functional projects in woodworking grabbed my attention at a young age, but I developed a passion for it during my high school "shop" class. I created several small projects and learned how to make use of the various tools of the trade in this class. Like everyone, I dreamed to do what I love and enjoy for a career so I then acquired a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Technology focusing on Woodworking. Currently, I work for Woodcraft selling all things woodworking related and using their workshop to develop my skills further and conducting classes for fellow enthusiasts.

So, browse the site... check out my work and experience, leave me some feedback on any work I may have completed for you or that you find in my portfolio, and feel free to contact me if you may have any questions regarding woodworking, classes I teach, or just to say "Hi!."
